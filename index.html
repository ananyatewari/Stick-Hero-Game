ABOUT THE GAME
Inspired from the original game itself, "Stick Hero" is an engaging and addictive 2D platformer game that challenges players to navigate a dynamic world of platforms and gaps using a unique and extendable stick. In this thrilling adventure, players take control of a nimble stick figure hero with the ability to elongate a stick at will. The objective is simple yet requires precision and timing i.e. extend the stick to bridge the gaps between platforms and reach new heights.

INSTRUCTIONS

Objective:
  • Extend the stick to bridge the gaps between platforms and reach higher levels.
Controls:
  • Hold down the mouse/Press down any key to start extending the stick
  • Release to stop extending the stick
MORE > >

Platform Navigation:

  • Time the stick extension to bridge the gap between platforms.
  • Successfully land the extended stick on the next platform to continue.

Game Over:

  • If the stick falls short and doesn't reach the next platform, the game ends.
  • Falling off the platforms at any point ends the game. You will have 60 seconds to do your magic

    • Restart:

  • After a is game over, players have the option to restart and try again.
